Privacy Policy — Our Day

Last updated: June 2026

Our Day is a wedding planning app developed by Guy Almog.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app may store, how it is used, and how users can contact us.

Information you provide

Our Day may store information that you choose to enter into the app, including:

  • Wedding details, such as names, date, venue, and planning notes
  • Guest information, such as names, phone numbers, RSVP status, guest groups, table assignments, meal choices, and guest type
  • Vendor information, such as vendor names, contact details, payments, and notes
  • Venue comparison details
  • Wedding checklist items and timeline events
  • Uploaded photos
  • Personal notes, thoughts, and planning information

The app only stores information that you choose to add.

Sign in with Apple

Our Day uses Sign in with Apple for account authentication. Apple may provide a user identifier and, depending on your Apple settings, your name and email address.

iCloud and CloudKit

Our Day uses Apple iCloud / CloudKit to store and sync your data across your Apple devices.

Your wedding planning data is stored in your private iCloud database associated with your Apple ID. The developer does not sell your data.

Photos

If you upload photos to the app, those photos may be stored in iCloud / CloudKit as part of your app data.

Notifications

Our Day may use local notifications for reminders such as checklist items, vendor due dates, or wedding timeline events. These notifications are generated on your device.

Data sharing

Partner sharing is currently not available in version 1.0. Future versions may support optional partner collaboration. If enabled in the future, sharing will only happen when the user chooses to invite another person.

Analytics

Our Day may store basic app usage or configuration information needed to operate and improve the app. We do not sell personal data to advertisers.

Account deletion

You can delete your account and app data from inside the app.

To delete your account:

  1. Open Our Day
  2. Go to More / Settings
  3. Choose Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

Deleting your account removes your app data from the app’s storage to the extent technically possible.

Data export

The app includes export options such as CSV guest export and JSON data export, so users can back up or review their data.

Children’s privacy

Our Day is intended for wedding planning and is not directed to children.
