Our Day Support Welcome to Our Day support. Our Day is a wedding planning app designed to help couples manage guests, seating, vendors, budget, checklist, wedding day timeline, notes, photos, and exports. Contact support For help, questions, bug reports, or account requests, contact: guyal253@gmail.com Please include: * A short description of the issue * Your device model * Your iOS version * The app version and build number * Screenshots if relevant Common topics Sign in Our Day uses Sign in with Apple. Make sure you are signed into iCloud on your device and that iCloud Drive is enabled. iCloud sync Our Day uses iCloud / CloudKit to store and sync your wedding planning data. If sync does not appear immediately, wait a few minutes and reopen the app. Guest import and export The app supports CSV guest import and export. You can also export a JSON backup of your app data.
