Common topics

Sign in

Our Day uses Sign in with Apple. Make sure you are signed into iCloud on your device and that iCloud Drive is enabled.

iCloud sync

Our Day uses iCloud / CloudKit to store and sync your wedding planning data. If sync does not appear immediately, wait a few minutes and reopen the app.

Guest import and export

The app supports CSV guest import and export. You can also export a JSON backup of your app data.

Delete account

You can delete your account from inside the app:

  1. Open Our Day
  2. Go to More / Settings
  3. Choose Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

Partner sharing

Partner sharing is temporarily unavailable in version 1.0 and will return in a future update after full sync support is ready.
